Hanging (un)chains

(November 2025 - Mendoza, Argentina)

.Climate Crisis .Reorientation of the organic legacy from fuel to dynamic shells emerges as a unique opportunity to address the projected doubling of residential buildings globally by 2050.

Hanging elasticos - bridges between our minds and the possibilities of matter - are instantiated in this series of interdisciplinary habitable spaces, inviting the exploration of a novel carbon ontology for reconnecting architecture theory and practice.
