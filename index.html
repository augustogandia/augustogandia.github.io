Augusto Gandia

Frameless Pivots (Carbon House, 2023)

This investigation explores the design of a set of elegant, lightweight carbon hinges as part of the prefabricated bespoke system of the Carbon House. As in the Shadow of Ledoux, the integral two-part design evokes a conversation of private and public spaces that emerge from the lightness of fiber-reinforced components, the movements of the machining process, and their disassembly. The design relates to the site through minimally invasive subtracted perforations that hold them into the wall panel without additional frames and through channels for gasket embedding. As such, the integral design maximizes daylight, guarantees air-tightness, and allows for disassembly.

Assamblage: Guerin, Gandia, Goulthorpe
