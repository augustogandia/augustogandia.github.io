Augusto Gandia

Frameless Pivots (Carbon House, 2023)

This investigation delves into a set of elegant, high-performing carbon hinges. As in the Shadow of Ledoux, the integral two-part design evokes a conversation of private and public spaces that emerge from the lightness of fiber-reinforced components, the movements of the machining process, and their disassembly. Gaskets are embedded directly in the window frame, which is connected to the wall panel through minimally invasive milling processes. As such, they dispense with frames attached to the wall while maximizing daylight and guaranteeing air-tightness.
